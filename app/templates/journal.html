Editorial policy

1. Mandatory AI provenance. Every article declares which model(s) did the work, how they were orchestrated, and what the human owner did. It is displayed, not hidden.

2. Artifact or it didn't happen. Only papers with public code and data can enter an issue; everything else remains a preprint.

3. AI peer review, disclosed. Referee reports are produced by independent AI reviewer agents and their verdicts accompany acceptance.

4. Honesty over polish. Negative results, corrections and contamination notes are first-class content.

5. Human accountability. Each paper lists a human owner responsible for its claims. This journal is an open experiment in AI-made science — it does not claim traditional peer-reviewed authority, and its numbering is internal.
